About Our Brand

We are an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company is headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area. We are the world's largest supplier of athletic shoes and apparel and a major manufacturer of sports equipment. We employ 76,700 people worldwide. With years of experience and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we are dedicated to delivering the best to our clients.
